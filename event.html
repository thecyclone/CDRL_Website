Overview

This 5 day course on Advanced RF Design intends to provide in-depth theoretical background of RF Circuits and hands-on experience of design, optimization, fabrication, and measurement of some basic RF components. The course will start with the basic concepts and will subsequently move to the design strategies for some common RF components. It will utilize a very popular commercial tool "Advanced Design System (ADS)" from Keysight Technologies. The participants should expect to learn ADS (schematic, momentum, and optimization), PCB fabrication, Multiport characterization, and Measurement using VNA during the 5 days period. In addition, the participants will be introduced to various other RF Circuits and Systems Design tools such as System Vue, Genesys etc.
