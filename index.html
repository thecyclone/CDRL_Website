About the Research Group

This research group works in the broad area of RF Electronics with particular emphasis on RF Measurements, RF and Mixed Signal Electronics, PA Design, Modeling and Linearization. Current research focus is in developing innovative design methodologies for multi-band RF/microwave circuits and systems for enabling optimal SDR applications and 'Green Communications'. In addition, we are also working on innovative solutions for implantable devices and their test & evaluation.
